Wisdom funeral, conveying the concept of green civilization

The life of the citizen is the key to people's livelihood.

The cause of the continuous pursuit of development and progress should have the meaning in the title.

In recent years, our city has been closely following the needs of the masses, making full use of advanced technologies such as the Internet, blockchain, cloud computing, big data, GPS, etc., in building a network service platform with good interactive experience, and implementing epidemic prevention and control security. Some exploration has been done.

"Internet + funeral" makes management safer and services more convenient

The spacious and bright service hall, the convenient inquiry system at your fingertips, and the detailed introduction to funeral etiquette... In the business hall of Nanjing funeral home, the one-stop funeral service here is impressive. In the meantime, the staff demonstrated the intelligent mobile "handheld service" platform.

"This platform covers the business handling of all aspects of the funeral service process, and realizes the information sharing and exchange of the transportation of remains, service negotiation, wake-up call, cremation, collection of ashes, etc. During the epidemic, if it is inconvenient to come to the hotel to handle funeral matters , you can use the platform to understand the basic information, charging standards, required application materials and other key elements of the funeral business, so as to facilitate the masses." The staff of the museum introduced.

This is just a microcosm of the city's smart funeral services for people's livelihood.

In accordance with the requirements of the national funeral and interment reform, our city adopts the construction model of "one platform, four architectures, and N applications" based on actual needs, strengthens the "one game of chess" idea of ​​integrated planning and multi-system development, and promotes cloud computing, physical All-round and deep integration of information technologies such as networking, big data, artificial intelligence and funeral service management, dig deep into the application needs of funeral work, and take solving practical problems as the starting point to promote cross-level, cross-regional, cross-system, and cross-departmental funerals Business linkage and resource sharing maximize the leading, leading and early warning role of funeral data.

Our city has also continued to deepen the reform of citizens' "one matter of death", further improve the efficiency of funeral work, meet the convenience needs of the masses to the greatest extent, and promote the one-stop joint service and integrated service of "one matter" for citizens' death, so as to realize the death of citizens "One thing" reduces materials, links, time, and running.

The relevant person in charge of the Municipal Funeral Management Office introduced that through the integration of the government, funeral homes, and cemetery multi-party funeral service resources, the research and development and design of the Nanjing funeral public service platform, the implementation of intelligent funeral services, the implementation of digital transformation, and the creation of a mass "hand-held service" platform, allowing the masses It is convenient, easy, and satisfying, so that scientific and technological achievements can benefit the daily life of the people.

Give full play to the advantages of scientific and technological integration to promote the ecological burial of the festival land

The construction of funeral and interment infrastructure should closely follow the pulse of the digital age and realize the characteristics of the times of the funeral business. Today, with the development of the Internet, various technological means emerge in an endless stream.

At present, the establishment of a virtual online space for the deceased is gradually being accepted by people, uploading images of the deceased, life introductions, important documents, etc. and related information of the deceased to the Internet to establish a virtual connection between the deceased and the living. The voice, face and smile of the deceased, and the inexhaustible condolences of relatives can be converted into multimedia files, which can be permanently stored and infinitely expanded, so that the deceased can live forever on the Internet.

"We have launched a digital life heritage library, which uses computer cloud space technology and digital means of Internet technology to retain the voices and smiles of the deceased. This has also become a supporting carrier for the ecological burial of Yuhua Gongde Garden, which is convenient for citizens to realize green online sacrifices. "The relevant person in charge of Yuhua Gongde Garden said.

Up to now, all major cemeteries in the city have opened up ecological burial areas, and there are more than 9,000 ecological burials in Yuhua Gongde Garden alone. Many of these cemeteries have established online memorial halls to provide online services, including helping users create biographies, offering services such as flower offerings, memory reviews and other services.

Technology is also an important driving force for funeral reform. Make good use of technology to make funerals no longer scary.

Always be with your family, condense all your thoughts in a small crystal... In 2020, Yuhua Gongde Garden officially launched a more eco-friendly burial: "Jing Jade of Life" burial, citizens can choose according to their own wishes The ashes of deceased relatives and friends are made into spherical crystals, carried or buried in the Gongde Garden.

The so-called "life crystal jade" is a kind of stable ashes crystal formed after the deceased's ashes are passed through a high temperature of about 1700 ℃ through modern technology, and some materials are appropriately added to burn. And weathering and deterioration, can be stored for a long time. To a certain extent, the "Jade of Life" of technological burial has boosted the ecological burial of the festival land.

Promote "cloud sacrifice sweeping", green civilization sacrifice sweeping has become common practice

"Qingming remembers the deceased, and sends condolences online."

At this critical period of epidemic prevention and control, in order to avoid large-scale gatherings of people, our city advocates family memorial services, online sacrifice sweeps and other methods, and vigorously promotes "cloud sacrifice sweeps".

This year, the municipal funeral department launched "Ning Sinian" version 3.0, re-optimized the user page, updated functions such as funeral information, service guide, business inquiry, etc., with a stronger sense of experience, and citizens can use this platform to perform online sacrifices at home.

Starting from March 10, citizens can access the "Ning Sinian" platform through the "My Nanjing" APP or the "Nanjing Funeral 96444" WeChat public account to conduct online sacrifice sweeps, online reservations, and leave messages to pray for blessings. After creating or logging in to the corresponding functional page, citizens can enter the information of the deceased, and use the forms of offering flowers, lighting candles, and sending messages to conduct online sacrifice sweeps.

According to statistics from the funeral department, since the launch of the "Ning Sinian" platform, millions of citizens have conducted online sacrifice sweeps. Using the Internet, green civilization sacrifices have become common practice. In the next step, the platform will also launch services such as business processing, deposit payment, and application for ecological burial awards.

The civil affairs department stated that the rapid development of digital technology has provided new opportunities for promoting the construction of funeral and interment informatization, and the use of informatization means to promote the reform and innovation of funeral and interment management services is a major issue of the times faced by current funeral practitioners. In order to realize the unified acceptance, one-stop service, and accurate collection and sharing of handling information for the masses, it is necessary to insist on creating a full-coverage, full-process digital integrated application intelligent system for funerals, and continuously improve the convenience and professionalism of funeral services. .
