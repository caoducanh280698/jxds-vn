Company Profile

Jiangxi Dongsheng Intelligent Equipment Group Co., Ltd.

It was founded on February 16, 2022 by the reorganization of elites engaged in the humanities funeral industry, equipment manufacturing and service. The company's factory area actually covers an area of 69,200 square meters, and the factory building area is 39,900 square meters. It has built domestic excellent funeral equipment manufacturing intelligent automatic Production line, and has a complete set of man-machine integrated control of production installation, quality inspection system. The comprehensive office R&D building has a construction area of 19,900 square meters and has invested more than 68 million yuan in fixed assets. It is a rookie enterprise in the professional service humanistic funeral industry that integrates design and manufacturing, product development, production, sales and one-stop service in the industry. The company is located in Zhangjiashan Industrial Park, Zhangshu City, Jiangxi Province, 2 kilometers away from the downtown area of Zhangshu, 10 kilometers away from the Zhangshu East Interchange entrance of Dongchang Expressway, and 1 km away from the Hujiafang Interchange entrance of Changzhang Expressway. The transportation is very convenient and the location advantage is obvious. .
