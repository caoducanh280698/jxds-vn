The "Funeral Management Regulations" plan to stipulate that the state establishes a basic public service system for funerals

China News Agency, Beijing, September 7 (Reporter Wang Zumin) On the 7th, the Ministry of Civil Affairs of China publicly solicited opinions on the "Funeral Management Regulations (Draft for Comments)" (hereinafter referred to as the revised draft). The revised draft intends to stipulate that the state establish a basic public service system for funerals, start China's funeral services more humanely, and further promote the reform of China's funerals.

China Funeral Association: Funeral should go to a more profound spiritual and value level

From October 28th to 29th, the 2020 China Funeral Association Cemetery Work Exchange Meeting was held. With the theme of "ecological integration and cultural co-creation", this exchange meeting invited more than 20 experts and scholars from inside and outside the industry to exchange ideas, opinions and practical experience. Experts called for the funeral work to be committed to safeguarding the dignity of life and building a humanistic, green, ecological and harmonious funeral service system. Funerals are no longer just the material level of body disposal, but should move towards a more profound spiritual level and value level.
